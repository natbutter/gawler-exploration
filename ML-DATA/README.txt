Training datasets are provided for each commodity.
They contain the known locations of commodity deposit with the underlying geology/geophysics at that location. 
Plus, an example of the randomly generated non-deposit data with these locations' underlying geology/geophysics.

Two target datasets are provided, which are the regularly sampled geology/geophysics grids across the Gawler Craton.
A low reolotuion version is provided as a *.csv
A high resolution version is provided as a *.zip
